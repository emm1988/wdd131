About Me

My name is Emmanuel, I am a student in the WDD 131 - Web Fundamentals Dynamic class. I am learning about web design and development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I was born in La Paz, Bolivia. I am a person who really like spend time outdoors, eating pizza and learning new things.

La Paz, Bolivia is a city of breathtaking contrasts and vibrant culture, perched high in the Andes at over 3,600 meters above sea level. Its dramatic setting offers stunning views from the iconic Mi Teleférico cable cars, which glide above colorful neighborhoods and bustling markets. The city blends indigenous traditions with colonial architecture, and places like the Witches’ Market and Valle de la Luna showcase its mystical charm and surreal landscapes.
